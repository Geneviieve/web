About

I'm a designer who loves the space where design meets emerging tech, making products that are not only beautiful, but built and validated to work.

Currently interning as a UI/UX Engineer at Taiwan Mobile, I design interfaces across multiple internal teams. Beyond design, I've also taken on a fair amount of QA work, testing on iOS devices and simulators and exploring test automation, which has given me a tester's eye and hands-on experience testing on iOS. Alongside, I'm finishing my Computer Science degree at BINUS University, specializing in Intelligent Systems.

My foundation is in data: Python, machine learning, and NLP, including a thesis on address normalization for Jakarta using a Small Language Model. I'm passionate about uncovering insights and turning them into experiences people genuinely understand.
