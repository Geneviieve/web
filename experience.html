UI/UX Engineer · Taiwan Mobile

Feb 2026 – Present
Internship · Taipei, Taiwan · works & delivers in English

At one of Taiwan's largest companies, my core role is designing UI/UX across several different internal teams. This is where I've gone deeper into UX itself: studying user flows to find the smoothest, most intuitive experience, while also thinking about the developers who build it so the designs stay realistic to implement. Because I work so closely with how things get built, I've also taken on a fair amount of QA and testing along the way, so I'm constantly adapting to new contexts and people.

What I do
  • Design UI/UX for multiple internal projects across different product teams.
  • Act as QA tester for an internal app, testing on iOS using Xcode, iOS simulators, and real devices.
  • Build test automation with Maestro, moving repetitive manual checks toward automated flows.
  • Write UAT (User Acceptance Testing) documentation to validate features before release.
  • Communicate and deliver across teams entirely in English.
What I learned

How real products get shipped and validated, not just designed. Working across teams taught me to adapt fast, ask the right questions, and bridge the gap between how something looks and how it behaves. The QA and automation work gave me a tester's eye for detail and real comfort with mobile and iOS testing.
